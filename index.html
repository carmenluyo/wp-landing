Nutririon

Why Grains ? - When looking for a carbohydrate to
keep your energy steady for hours as well as you feel-
ing full; reach for a complex carbohydrate. Complex
carbohydrates include short chain carbohydrates for a
rapid energy boost as well as long chain, which take
longer to digest therefore releasing energy over time.
Typically CC’s
are high in dietary fiber. For grains; having all three parts:
germ, endosperm and bran, intact-gives an assortment of different long
and short carbohydrates giving you long lasting energy and fullness to
tackle your day. On-top of long lasting energy some of these grains also
have substantial amounts of the four substances most Americans do not
consume enough of: calcium, iron, protein and fiber. Here are our op-
tions for a longer lasting carbohydrate:


Brown Rice-
Eaten in place of white rice, brown rice qualifies as a good
source of fiber and a good source of iron. Due to its bran, endosperm
and germ staying intact brown rice is chewier in texture and nuttier in
flavor than its white rice counterpart.


Bulgur-
Consumed often in European, Middle Eastern and Indian influ-
enced cuisines, bulgur qualifies as a good source of iron, protein, and
one cup contains nearly 75% of daily dietary fiber. It too has a slight
nutty flavor and chewy texture.

VISION
Our Vision is to become the most eco-friendly and sustainable restau-
rant in midtown, and to be recognized for having the best quality ingredi-
ents while continuing to exceed our customer’s
MISSION
We use high quality ingredients, provide healthy meals that are afford-
able so you can feel good about eating the best in locally sourced, farm
fresh and seasonal foods.
RESPONSIBILITY
Market Crates provides locally grown greens and grains so you can
have a better and richer lifestyle. We believe that sustainability is very im-
portant to the food industry and for the environment. We also use LED
lights, biodegradable trays and composting.
